Bringing live music back to Amsterdam Oost

Nurturing and promoting live music, performance and cultural activities in Amsterdam Oost in a sustainable, inclusive, intercultural, innovative, and accessible way. Stichting Oost Rules! will host pop-up live music events in the upcoming months with the ultimate goal of opening a live music venue and cultural center in the Amsterdam Oost.

Why open a live music venue in Oost?

There are currently no* dedicated live music venues in this area of Amsterdam that once had many performance and rehearsal spaces and where many musicians still live. For an illustration of the issue, see our interactive live music venue mapping project (in development)HERE .

Who we are

We love Oost and we love Music

We are a group of neighbors and regulars of Bar Joost, musicians and music lovers who became friends over the years, frequently lamenting the lack of live music in the neighborhood. Our neighborhood is under served in cultural activities and while a few great organizations are doing community oriented work, none of them are focusing on live music. We want to change that.

The Name

Origins

In the early 2000s, one of our early supporters and former neighbor (sound engineer and musician, Remko Visser) moved to Oost. His friends questioned the move to such an uncool neighborhood so a group of musicians in the area made Oost Rules! t-shirts in defense.

Big Dreams

But starting small

We want to build a place where performers will get decent pay, qualified tech staff hired, front of house jobs provided to locals, rehearsal and community space offered for affordable fees and local talent can be nurtured. But in the meantime we will host pop-up events in the area.

Community

For, and by, the community

We want to reflect the wide diversity of the neighborhood and will partner with locals first and experts second. Programming will focus on local artists and build off of the communities that reside in Oost, while expanding cultural offerings in general. *there are a few, but they have limited programming and/or cater to a very specific audience

Impact

Not just for musicians and music lovers

While our core mission is to nurture local live musicians and provide them with opportunities to perform, Oost Rules! will program storytelling nights, visual art, provide space to other community groups, work with local promoters and much more! Our expansion plans to offer rehearsal space and a repair shop will bring additional benefits as well.
